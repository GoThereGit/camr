What's New?

  • CAMRP 2023
    • 2,000 interrogative sentences added as Test C (Blind Test).
  • CAMRP 2022
    • 1,999 extra sentences added as Test B (Blind Test).
  • MRP 2020
    • Concept alignment evaluation included.
  • CAMR 2.0
    • Developed by Brandeis University & Nanjing Normal University.
    • Semantic representations of a set of approximately 20,000 Chinese sentences from Chinese Treebank (CTB) 8.0.
  • CAMR Annotation Toolkit 2.0
    • Alignment of concept and relation annotated.
  • CAMR 1.0
    • Based on the annotation methodology developed for English with adaptations for handling specific Chinese phenomena.
  • The Little Prince Corpus 1.0 (Chinese Translation)
    • Sentences aligned to ISI's English translation version.
  • CAMR Annotation Toolkit 1.0
    • In sync with ISI's style.
